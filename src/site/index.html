How To Play

Rules

Objective

The objective of the game is to be the last man standing. Each player has a base as well as occupied spaces. Once a players base is destroyed, they are out of the game.

The Game Board

The game board is an infinitely repeating square. If a piece moves off the edge of the board, it is placed at the opposite end of the board.

To start the game each player has a base with 10 points of material. Players move pieces to occupy neighboring squares. Occupied spaces must have a path to their respective base.

Moving and Attacking

A player may move their peices at any time. A player may move any amount of their peices to an adjacent square if it is unoccupied or is already occupied by them. The cursor displays the amount of pieces being moved.

If the player moves onto a square occupied by an opponent, they attack. Each point used in the attack destroys a point on the contested square. If all points on the contested square are destroyed it will become unoccupied.

Alliances

If the player moves onto a square occupied by an ally they render aid. Rather than attacking, points are simply transferred between players.

Recieving Points

Every 30-45 seconds each occupied square recieves one additional point.
